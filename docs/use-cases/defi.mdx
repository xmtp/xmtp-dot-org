#### Inspiration

- **Transaction confirmations**: Confirming transactions securely and privately, adding an extra layer of trust.

- **Notifications**: Sending notifications related to trades, liquidity pools, or any significant market movement.

- **Communication within DeFi platforms**: Allowing seamless interaction between different decentralized applications and platforms.

- **Facilitate DeFi operations within group chats**: Group-wide participation in liquidity pools and collective decision-making for DeFi strategies.
